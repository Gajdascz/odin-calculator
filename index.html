The Odin Project: Calculator

by Nolan Gajdascz

Calculator Information

  • This calculator will always round to the nearest 10^20 decimal place.
  • Each key is mapped to it's respective button.
  • Shift+key must be pressed to enter +,*,!,%,^.
  • All Clear (AC) is mapped to Escape.
  • Back is mapped to Backspace.
  • Modulo (MOD) is mapped to m.
